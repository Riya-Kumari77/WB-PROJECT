Borrowing Services

Our library offers a range of borrowing services, including physical books, magazines, and audiobooks.

You can borrow books for up to 3 weeks, magazines for 1 week, and audiobooks for 2 weeks. Renewals are available online.

Digital Resources

Access a wide range of digital resources including eBooks, audiobooks, and online journals.

Our digital library is available 24/7. You can access eBooks and audiobooks via our mobile app or website.
